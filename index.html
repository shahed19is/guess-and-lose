Guess & Lose

a game by SHAHED

Instructions

  • It is a guessing game.
  • You can know about your six-sense
  • A random number between 0 to 9 will be generated autometically
  • This random number will be hidden
  • Player has to guess a number and submit it
  • After that palyer has to flip the random numbered card
  • If player guessed the number correctly then he/she will be win
  • Otherwise player will be lost.
